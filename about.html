Who Are We?

VKB Orchards is a family-run orchard located in the beautiful hills of Himachal Pradesh. We are dedicated to growing fresh, natural, and chemical-free apples with care and tradition.

Our Story

What started as a small family orchard has grown into a trusted name for premium-quality apples. With hard work and passion, we now deliver fresh fruits directly from our orchards to customers’ homes.

  • Direct farm-to-customer delivery
